Get To Know Us

Welcome to Ironclåd, a clothing revolution at fast fashion prices! Our company strives to provide fashionable and stylish apparel, while still maintaining ethical and sustainability standards (unlike many of our competitors). Have a look below to get acquainted with our brand and our passion for fashion!

Our garments are sewn with only the best of materials. We produce our own threads and fabrics, always 100% cotton or soft wool. And, by combining these materials with modern textile techniques and machinery, we manage to produce high quality clothing at bargain prices. Some special pieces also include other fabrics, such as our silk flowing summer dresses; however, these pieces are typically seasonal and in limited quantities. That's why we definitely recommend that you sign up for our newsletter below in order to always get notified on new deals and offers.

By using advanced tools and apparatuses, our tailors work efficiently and precisely and no stitch is sewn out of its appropriate spot. And, even in the rare cases when a mistake does happen, our rigorous quality control ensures that they are rectified before any piece of garment is placed into your hands. You can even see some clips of our production procedure in the video to the right! If that isn't enough to convince you to give us a try, just learn about our rich history down below!

Our first shop was opened way back in 1964 on the corner of a small decrepit building in the suburbs of Stockholm. After dressing one of the most well-known socialites of old Sweden, the new-found popularity allowed us to upgrade and open up a store much closer to the heart of the capital. Since then, not only have we launched two new stores in Sweden, but also expanded abroad to other countries - namely, Bulgaria and Albania! With three locations running in each country, our darling customers can find a shop nearby without much hassle!
